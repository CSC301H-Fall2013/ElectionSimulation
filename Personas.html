Election Master, Jonathan Edwards

Age:35

Jonathan Edwards is a 35-year-old, male, Election Master. He is more of a behind-the-scenes kind of worker, focused, always determined, and very skilled. He is also usually very underappreciated. Jonathan is married to a lovely wife of the same age, with no children to his name. She is one of the few people who truly appreciates his efforts. Jonathan lives with his wife in a flat in downtown Toronto where when he arrives after a long day of work has a very comfortable, relaxing evening with his wife. Jonathan is mostly estranged from his family and his parents are unfortunately no longer alive. His family drifted after their departures when they got into a feud over his parents' will. Jonathan is content to not have to be around what he calls his 'money-sucking relatives'.

His hobbies include disassembling software and reading up on Machine Learning ever since he started to study the topic. Jonathan initially accepted his job as his party's Election Master because of the appreciation and responsibilities he assumed being a 'master' would afford him. He was wrong, it was really a fluffed up title for a lowly technician to configure the technology for his party. Strangely enough, he tried to avoid politics most of his life anyways, but jumped at the opportunity to help people where his surrounding colleagues saw human virtue as political vice. Jonathan works full time: a total of 65 hours every week. The pay is good. No matter how much his underappreciated self gets battered after a long work shift, he knows not to transfer his anguish to his wife. He's been working at the position for three years, with hopes of moving up the ranks with his new-found knowledge of how politics work.

Jonathan is very fond of technology. He became computer literate at a very young age, anticipating that it would change the way everything gets done, and boy was he right.. He is also very skilled with computers and became relatively good at disassembling software so much that he can modify underlying assembly code and still yet understand what he's doing. Since Jonathan is so skilled with application dis-assembly, he never really needs too much of an accessible GUI he only desires that the parameters that he needs to be specified can be easily inferred. In this case, as the Election Master, he would like a system that he could use to accurately predict a mapping of his party's effort to the results at the polls with an easily-specifiable and thorough set of parameters. Though Jonathan likes reading on Machine Learning, he is still new to the topic so he would need this prediction model to be preprogrammed.

Player/Presenter, Linda Johnson née Daniels

Age 45

Linda is 50 years old and a lifelong Canadian citizen. She grew up in Oakville, Ontario, but has lived closer to downtown Toronto since graduating high school. She graduated from York University with a degree in Sociology and has a keen interest in politics.

Linda has been happily married for sixteen years to Derek, an accountant for a major Canadian bank. She had been employed full-time as an administrator for a small non-profit organization until the birth of their only child, Matthew, age 14. She chose to quit, in light of the high expense and low availability of quality daycare in the city. She enjoys having lots of quality time with her son, but would be open to returning to work full-time if the opportunity arose. Linda’s parents still live in Oakville, and enjoy it when Linda and her family visit on the long weekends. Like her son, Linda was an only child.

Linda has a very outgoing personality. She enjoys meeting new people and trying new things. She values the relationships she made at university, and still makes time to meet with her old friends on a regular basis. Now that her son is a little more self-sufficient, Linda has taken advantage of an opportunity to work part-time for her political party of choice. Her job is to present statistical data to politicians and public figures, in an effort to show the merits of electoral reform in Canada. This job plays to her public speaking and communication skills, and she’s relished the chance to advocate for something that she holds quite dear.

Linda wakes up at 7:00 each morning to help get Derek and Matthew on their way. She packs a lunch for each of them after a cup of coffee and a brief look through that day’s newspaper. After they’ve left, she does the grocery shopping and plans dinner for the evening. She hates doing the shopping after work, as the lines are always significantly longer. Unless there is a meeting or presentation to attend, she aims to arrive at work at 10:00 each morning, and stay until around 4. She spends the majority of her day arranging meetings and working with the “election master” to build specialized presentationsfor each of them. After coming home and preparing dinner with help from her husband, she has a good amount free time in the evening for reading and watching television.

Linda has a positive relationship with technology. She was a reluctant adopter of personal computers but values the convenience of things like email and Office applications. She recently upgraded to a smartphone, and is gradually adapting to the touchscreen controls. She values ease of use and user-friendliness in her applications, and isn’t too proud to ask for help when her electronic devices are experiencing problems, but she is often able to figure things out on her own. She has a tablet, and enjoys it for reading books.

Linda needs an application with a simple, user-friendly interface and, more importantly, she needs it to produce attractive graphics and represent data in an easy-to-understand fashion. This is more for the sake of her audience than for her.

Implementer: Jennifer Crawford

Age 43

Jennifer Crawford is a 43-year-old female who has spend most of her years dealing with software. She likes to be right, and she will do everything to make it right. She is very confident with her skills but she is shy when it comes with dealing with real people. Jennifer is happily married with 5 months older husband, with 2 children currently in high school. She has a great passion in cooking food, so she packed up their lunch every day with different dishes of their favourites. They live in upper town, a quieter place where Jennifer thought that it was better to raise their kids. However, as the kids are going to university really soon, they are considering moving towards downtown area. The budget is not so enough since they want a bigger house with guest rooms for family relatives who come by every Christmas to have a family reunion.

Her hobbies include reading newly established software and programming languages as she wants to remain competitive with the younger generations. Jennifer is now an implementer for the Election software. This job is perfect for her since she can always see the outcome of the simulation with the real world result, and she will implement more features or adjust the current settings when they don’t match. However, Jennifer cannotcare less for the politics, she spend all of her free time with colleagues chatting about the new technologies that just came out, or a nice place for her kids to have a good vacation. She works 40 hours per week, exactly 8 hours a day. She doesn’t mind to stay overtime, but her ability and dedication always let her finish the work before it is due. As a result, she seldom has any argument with her boss or colleagues, and she loves to keep it this way.

Jennifer is a high level programmer, she sometimes writes small apps for fun, but none of them can earn her a million yet. She is very skilled with computers and sometimes she can even add her own features to the programs that have bugs or need it. Recently, she is learning a new way to do the simulation, a more graphical representation and easy-to-learn for people who is not a computer scientist. As building from the ground will take too much work, she is searching for an existing model that has been built by someone else, and wants to establish it from there. This might be a little challenging for her, but Jennifer is ready to conquer it.
